## Schedule Business

**Purpose**  
Serves timetable consumption use cases, basket management, and schedule exports.

**Main responsibilities**
- Assemble read models for timetable views.
- Maintain basket state and apply modifications.
- Export schedule in requested format. (ics/pdf/csv)

**Collaborations**
- Supplies projections and accepts changes from `Schedule HTML`.
- Exchanges collision feedback and availability checks with `Scheduling Business`.
- Reads from and writes to `Schedules DB`.
- Exports data to `Calendar Clients`
