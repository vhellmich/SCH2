## Schedule HTML

**Purpose**  
Delivers the timetable and basket GUI for students and teachers.

**Main responsibilities**
- Render weekly schedules, and basket status in an interactive view.
- Allow users to adjust selections, compare alternatives, and switch timeframes.
- Initiate exports and reflect backend updates immediately in the interface.

**Collaborations**
- Retrieves time table data, writes changes, and requests exports from `Schedule Business`.
