**Purpose**  
Provides a unified platform for subject management, timetable planning, publication, and reporting across the university.

**Main responsibilities**  
- Support teachers in defining subjects, requirements, and preferred times.  
- Enable the scheduling committee to plan and validate timetables while avoiding conflicts.  
- Offer students and teachers clear timetable views, basket planning, and exports.  
- Deliver managers the analytics needed to balance capacity with student demand.  
- Secure access through shared authentication, authorisation, and audit services.

**Key collaborations**  
- Integrates with `University SSO` for sign-in and identity assertions.  
- Sends workflow notifications via `Notification Service`.  
- Publishes calendar feeds to external `Calendar Clients`.
